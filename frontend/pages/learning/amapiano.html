Amapiano

Born in South Africa in the 2010s. Faster, bouncier, and piano-driven.

Key Facts

🎵 BPM

~112 — faster than Afrobeat, bouncy

🥁 Drum

Log drum — deep rolling BOOM-tss

🎹 Keys

Piano keys float above the groove

📍 Origin

South Africa, 2010s

Reference Track

Mnike — Tyler ICU & Uncle Waffles
