Genre 01 · Afrobeat

Afrobeat

Born in Nigeria in the 1970s. Mid-tempo, syncopated, and vocal-forward.

Key Facts

🎵 BPM

~95 — mid-tempo, swung groove

🥁 Drum

Talking drum — syncopated hits

🎤 Vocals

Call-and-response storytelling

📍 Origin

Nigeria, 1970s (Fela Kuti lineage)

Reference Track

Heis — Rema

🎵 ~95 BPM — feel this first
Then notice: talking drum — do-do-do-DUM syncopated hit
