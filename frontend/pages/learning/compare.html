Afrobeat vs Amapiano

Bring both lessons together and focus on the differences most likely to matter when you hear a track.

Side-by-Side Guide

What To Remember

Start with tempo and feel. Amapiano usually feels faster and more floating. Afrobeat usually feels more grounded and live.

Then listen for the clue instrument. Talking drum points you toward Afrobeat. Log drum and airy piano point you toward Amapiano.

Finally notice the space of the track. Afrobeat often feels stage-like and percussive. Amapiano often feels immersive and club-like.
